About us

We make the best pizzas in Finand! Our pizzas are made with fresh and very high quality ingredients.
We've been nominated Finlands best pizzeria since 2018 - present.

About our family

The Angeli family has moved to finland during the Helsinki olympics in 1952. We were collaborators in a small icecream factory that still exists. During the years the icecream was left behind by my grandfather and he started to focus on preparing statues, that was the old family tradition. I started to collect recepies and ideas during the summer visits to relatives in tuscany. From there came up the idea to share the enjoyable moments with friends around excellent food. In 2017 I decided to share a big treasure also with others establishing a pizzeria/trattoria.

About the restaurant

Angeli's pizza! was founded in 2017 with the business idea to make tasty quality pizzas and delicius portions made with basic tuscanian family recepies. We use high quality Italian ingredients that we import ourselfes. Our owen i heated by wood so the pizzas get a special original taste. In our pizzeria, you can find excellent food and a friendly service. Please come and visit us and feel like home!
